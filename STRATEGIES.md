## The Remaining Doctrines (57–108)

Doctrines 57–108 are **combinatorial variants and hybrids** of the above families, deployed in the phased pipeline of the ESTRELLA v14 engine:

- **57–66:** PUA variants with different mutation rates (1, 2, 3, 4, 5 positions per trial)
- **67–76:** Storm variants with different column counts (2, 3, 4) and blame thresholds
- **77–86:** Ghost variants with different co-occurrence scoring functions
- **87–93:** Triple Attack on all top-scoring triples (not just {2,4,20})
- **94–100:** Hill Climbing with different restart thresholds (100, 250, 500, 1000 stagnation)
- **101–104:** Hybrid strategies (Ghost → Heat-Seeker, Storm → Pitbull, etc.)
- **105–108:** Architect rotation — the engine cycles through all families in fixed order
